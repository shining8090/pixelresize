WebP vs JPG: Which Image Format is Better for Web?

WebP is a modern image format that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. JPG, on the other hand, is the most common format for photos.

How to resize an image without losing quality?

Answer: You can resize an image without losing quality by using a lossless format like PNG or WebP and maintaining aspect ratio. This ensures that the image remains sharp and clear even after resizing.
