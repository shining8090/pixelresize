PixelResize Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

Quick Privacy Notice: PixelResize is 100% online. Your images are processed directly in your browser, never uploaded, stored, or viewed by us. All operations happen locally on your device for complete privacy and security.

1. Introduction

At PixelResize, accessible from https://pixelresize.site, protecting your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information while you use our website and services. By using PixelResize, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Critical Privacy Notice: PixelResize operates entirely in your browser. All image processing, resizing, and conversion happens locally on your device. Your images are never uploaded, stored, or viewed by us.

2. What We Do Not Collect

PixelResize does not collect, store, or process your image files or personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not upload your images to any server.
  • We do not store your images in any database.
  • We do not keep logs of filenames, image metadata, or content.
  • We do not use your images for analytics, advertising, or machine learning.

All image data stays on your device and is removed from memory when you close your browser tab.

3. Information We Collect

PixelResize does not collect personal information from users. We may only collect technical, non-identifiable data automatically via your browser to help our website function properly, such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Approximate region (from IP for security or troubleshooting purposes only)
  • Page visits and clicks without linking them to individuals

This information is used solely for improving the website and troubleshooting issues. No personally identifiable information is stored.

4. Cookies and Analytics

PixelResize may use non-identifying browser cookies for functionality such as remembering site preferences. We do not use cookies to track users across other websites.

We do not serve third-party advertisements on our website. Our tools are free to use and do not require any tracking for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Privacy Policy applies only to PixelResize. Third-party ad networks or services may have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them if you interact with external services through our site.

6. Data Security

Since PixelResize does not store your images or personal information, the risk of data compromise is virtually nonexistent. All processing occurs locally on your device.

We follow best practices to maintain website security and integrity but do not store sensitive user data.

7. Children’s Information

PixelResize does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise online activity. If you believe a child has shared personal information through our site, please contact us to ensure it is not stored.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our services. Updates are effective immediately upon posting. Check this page periodically for the latest version.
