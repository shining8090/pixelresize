Contact Us

At PixelResize, we are committed to providing the best possible experience for our users. Whether you have a question about our tools, a suggestion for a new feature, or you've encountered a bug that needs squashing, we want to hear from you. Your feedback is the primary driver of our development roadmap, and we take every message seriously.

Our team is passionate about building high-quality, privacy-focused image tools, and we believe that open communication with our community is essential to achieving our mission. Please don't hesitate to reach out—we're here to help!

Why Contact Us?

We value all types of communication from our users. Here are some of the most common reasons people get in touch with the PixelResize team:

  • Feature Requests: Is there an image tool you need that we don't offer yet? Let us know! We are always looking to expand our suite of browser-based tools.
  • Bug Reports: If a tool isn't working as expected in your specific browser or with a certain file type, your report helps us fix it for everyone.
  • Partnerships: Are you interested in collaborating with PixelResize or integrating our tools into your workflow? We're open to discussing professional opportunities.
  • General Feedback: Even if you just want to say "thanks" or tell us how PixelResize helped you with a project, we love hearing from our users!

Support FAQ

How long does it take to get a response?

We strive to respond to all emails as quickly as possible. Generally, you can expect a reply within 1 to 2 business days. During periods of high volume or holidays, it may take slightly longer, but we guarantee that every legitimate inquiry will receive a human response.

Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we only offer support via email. This allows us to keep our services free for everyone and ensures that we can provide detailed, technical assistance by sharing links, screenshots, and code snippets when necessary.

Can I request a custom tool for my business?

We are always open to hearing about specific needs. While we prioritize tools that benefit the widest range of users, we are happy to discuss custom requirements or potential enterprise solutions. Send us an email with your project details!
