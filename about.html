About PixelResize

Our Story

PixelResize is a privacy-focused online platform that provides fast and simple image tools directly in your browser. Our mission is to make image resizing, compression, and conversion easy without requiring software downloads, account registration, or server uploads.

Many online image tools require users to upload files to remote servers. This raises concerns about privacy, security, and slow processing times. PixelResize was created to solve this problem by using modern browser technologies that process images locally on your device.

This means your images stay completely under your control.

Privacy-First Image Processing

PixelResize is built with a privacy-first architecture. All tools run directly in your web browser using technologies like JavaScript, HTML5 Canvas, and modern browser APIs.

When you use PixelResize:

  • Your images are never uploaded to our servers
  • Your files are never stored or saved
  • Your images are never viewed or analyzed by us
  • All processing happens locally on your device

This approach provides maximum privacy and extremely fast performance. Once you close the browser tab, any temporary processing data is automatically removed from memory.

Our Image Tools

PixelResize offers a growing set of lightweight tools designed to help users optimize images for websites, social media, design projects, and digital content.

Popular tools include:

  • Image Crop Tool – Crop images easily for perfect framing
  • Metadata Remover – Strip EXIF data for privacy
  • JPG to PNG Converter – Convert JPEG images to PNG format
  • PNG to JPG Converter – Convert PNG files into JPEG images
  • JPG to WebP Converter – Optimize images for modern web performance
  • GIF to WebP Converter – Convert animated GIFs to efficient WebP format
  • WebP to JPG Converter – Convert modern images to widely supported formats

These tools are designed to work instantly in your browser without uploads or delays.
