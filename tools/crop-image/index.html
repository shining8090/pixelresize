How to Crop Images Online

Cropping your photos is simple and secure with PixelResize. Whether you are preparing a Discord profile picture or a YouTube thumbnail, our tool is the perfect choice. Follow these steps:

  1. Upload: Drop your image into the cropper or click "Select Image".
  2. Select Area: Drag the handles on the image to select the area you want to keep.
  3. Choose Ratio: Use our preset aspect ratios like 1:1 for Instagram, 16:9 for YouTube, or custom for passport photos.
  4. Crop: Click the "Crop Image" button.
  5. Download: Your cropped image is ready! Click "Download Cropped Image" to save it.
