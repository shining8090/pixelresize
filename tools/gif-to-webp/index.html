Why Convert GIF to WebP?

WebP is a modern image format that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. When you convert gif to webp, you can achieve significantly smaller file sizes while maintaining or even improving visual quality.

Animated WebP supports 24-bit color and 8-bit transparency, compared to GIF's limited 8-bit color and 1-bit transparency. This means your animate gif to webp conversion will result in smoother gradients and cleaner edges.

Benefits of Animated WebP

  • Smaller File Size: WebP animations are typically 20-30% smaller than equivalent GIFs.
  • Better Quality: Supports millions of colors instead of just 256.
  • Alpha Transparency: Superior transparency handling for complex backgrounds.
  • SEO Friendly: Faster loading times improve your Core Web Vitals and search rankings.
