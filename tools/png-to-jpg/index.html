Why Convert PNG to JPG?

Choosing the right format is key for online image optimization. Here is why you might prefer JPG:

  • Smaller File Size: JPG uses efficient lossy compression, which significantly reduces file size compared to lossless PNG.
  • Better for Photos: JPG is the standard format for photographs and complex images on the web.
  • Universal Compatibility: JPG is supported by devices and platforms like WhatsApp and Instagram.
  • Faster Loading: Small JPG files help your website load quicker, improving SEO rankings.
