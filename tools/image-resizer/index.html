How to Use the Image Resizer

Our online image resizer is designed to be fast, simple, and private. Follow these steps to resize your images in seconds:

  1. Upload: Drag and drop your image into the upload area or click the "Select Image" button to browse your device.
  2. Configure: Enter your desired width and height in pixels. By default, the aspect ratio is locked to prevent your image from looking stretched.
  3. Select Format: Choose your preferred output format. You can convert your image to WebP, JPG, or PNG while resizing.
  4. Apply: Click "Apply Changes" to see the estimated file size.
  5. Download: Once you're happy with the settings, click "Download Image" to save the optimized file to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our online image resizing tool.

How do I resize an image without losing quality?

PixelResize uses professional resampling filters to maintain maximum sharpness. For best results, avoid upscaling small images to much larger dimensions.

Are my images uploaded to any server?

No. All processing happens in your browser's RAM. Your original image and the resized version never leave your computer.

What image formats are supported?

We support standard web formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, AVIF, BMP, and GIF. You can also convert between these formats while resizing.

Is there a file size limit?

The only limit is your device's available memory. Most modern browsers can easily handle images up to 50MB or 8K resolution.
