How to Compress Images Online

Our image compressor uses smart lossy compression to reduce the file size of your images while keeping them looking great. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload: Drag your image into the box or click "Select Image".
  2. Adjust Quality: Use the slider to set your desired quality level. 80% is usually the "sweet spot" for web images.
  3. Select Format: For the best compression, we recommend converting your images to WebP.
  4. Preview: Click "Compress Image" to calculate the new file size.
  5. Download: Save your optimized image instantly.

Why Compress Your Images?

  • Boost Site Speed: Smaller images load faster, reducing your bounce rate.
  • Save Storage: Free up space on your server or cloud storage.
  • Better SEO: Google rewards fast-loading websites with higher rankings.
  • Mobile Friendly: Optimized images save data for your mobile users.
