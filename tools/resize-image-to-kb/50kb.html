How to Resize Image to 50KB

Many online applications and government forms require images to be under 50KB. Achieving this small file size while maintaining legibility can be tricky. Here is how to do it:

  • Reduce Dimensions: Smaller pixel dimensions lead to smaller file sizes. Try 800x800 or 1000x1000 pixels.
  • Lower Quality: Adjust the quality slider. For 50KB, you might need to go as low as 70-80%.
  • Use WebP: WebP offers better compression than JPG, helping you reach 50KB with better quality.
