Why Convert JPG to WebP?

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. Reducing your page load time is critical, and a photo size reducer like our WebP converter is the best way to achieve it. Whether you are using image resizer or image compressor, always consider WebP for the final output.

  • Smaller files: WebP lossy images are 25-34% smaller than comparable JPG images. This is perfect for compressing images under 50KB.
  • Better SEO: Faster images lead to better Core Web Vitals and higher search rankings.
  • Transparency: WebP supports transparency (alpha channel) while maintaining small sizes, making it a great alternative to PNG.
  • Universal Support: All modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge support WebP.
