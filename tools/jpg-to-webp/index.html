Why Convert JPG to WebP?

  • Superior Compression: WebP files are typically 25-35% smaller than JPGs at the same quality.
  • Faster Load Times: Smaller files mean faster websites and better user experience.
  • Better SEO: Google recommends using modern image formats like WebP for better rankings.
  • Modern Standard: WebP is supported by all modern browsers and is the future of web imagery.
