What is the best size for a YouTube Thumbnail?

According to YouTube's official recommendations, your thumbnail should have a resolution of 1280x720 (with a minimum width of 640 pixels) and maintain a 16:9 aspect ratio. Additionally, the file size must be under 2MB.

How to Make the Perfect YouTube Thumbnail

  1. Upload: Start with your high-quality graphic or photo.
  2. Dimensions: Set width to 1280 and height to 720.
  3. Compression: If your file is over 2MB, use our **image compressor** to reduce it while keeping it sharp.
  4. Format: JPEG, PNG, or GIF are supported. We recommend JPEG for thumbnails.
