Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about resizing and optimizing images.

How can I resize an image online for free?

Simply upload your image to PixelResize, set your desired dimensions, and click download. It's 100% free and requires no registration.

Is it safe to upload my images to PixelResize?

Yes! PixelResize uses browser-based processing, meaning your images are never uploaded to our servers. They stay on your device throughout the entire process.

Does resizing an image reduce its quality?

Our tool uses advanced resampling algorithms to ensure your images remain as sharp as possible. However, significant upscaling may result in some loss of clarity.

What is the best format for web images?

WebP and AVIF are generally the best formats for the web as they offer superior compression compared to JPG and PNG.
